The differentiator

Every engagement, measured in ROAI.

Return on AI Investment is the metric that runs through the whole platform — hours saved plus revenue uplift, divided by what the AI cost. It's on every client card, every deep dive, and the customer's own portal.

Because the number is consistent across the portfolio and the customer view, FPT and the client are always looking at the same source of truth — which makes renewals and expansion a data conversation, not a debate.

Not a mockup

Live usage-based billing, wired to Metronome.

The Billing → Live panel reads a real Metronome account through a secure, read-only Worker proxy — the API key never reaches the browser. Customers, contracts, commits, credits, usage drawdown and invoices are all real billing objects.

What's live
  • 7 customers on prepaid-commit (Enterprise/Gov) or hybrid (platform fee + usage) models
  • Finalized + draft invoices with real totals — and 6 months of revenue history
  • Commit/credit balances drawing down against metered usage
  • A full offering catalog: billable metrics, products, rate cards and packages
Why it matters

It proves the commercial model is real, not painted on: usage-based pricing, committed-spend drawdown, government Net-60/PO flows and self-serve top-ups all run on production billing infrastructure. The same proxy pattern drops into a real deployment unchanged.
